About Me

Skilled hands.
A present heart.

I've been doing this work since May 2009 — not because it's a career, but because I genuinely believe in what skilled, intentional touch can do. Over 16 years and thousands of sessions, I've developed an approach that's intuitive, evidence-informed, and deeply personal.

My work goes beyond muscle and fascia. I work with the whole person — the physical patterns your body has held onto, and the emotional ones too. Whether you're here for pain relief, athletic recovery, or something harder to name, I'll meet you exactly where you are.

Myokinetic Touch is a space where your body gets to be heard, not just treated.

What I Offer

Work that meets
your body where it is

Every session starts with a real conversation about what's going on for you. From there, I draw on whatever combination of techniques will actually help — not a menu, a response.

Relaxation

Swedish Massage

Long, flowing strokes and rhythmic kneading to ease generalized tension, improve circulation, and bring your nervous system back to baseline.

Therapeutic

Deep Tissue

Slow, deliberate work into deeper muscle layers and fascia — addressing chronic tension patterns, adhesions, and the postural imbalances that build over time.

Performance

Sports Massage

Pre- and post-event work to prepare your body for performance, speed recovery, and reduce injury risk — for competitive athletes and weekend warriors alike.

Specialized

Prenatal Massage

Gentle, pregnancy-appropriate techniques to relieve back pain, reduce swelling, and support your overall wellbeing throughout every stage of pregnancy.

Structural

Myofascial Release

Sustained, gentle pressure to release fascial restrictions, restore range of motion, and ease pain throughout the connective tissue network that holds everything together.

Restorative

Lymphatic Drainage

Very light, rhythmic work following the body's lymphatic pathways to reduce inflammation, support immune function, and accelerate recovery — including post-surgical healing.

Mind · Body · Soul

Somatic Work

A deeper invitation — to connect with what your body has been wanting to say all along. Somatic work is a visit into the past, present, and future. It bridges the body to the mind and the soul, gently surfacing what's been held in the tissue so it can finally move.
