About Me

Precision, presence, and purpose

My journey into massage therapy began in May 2009, growing into a full-time practice in 2010. Over 16 years of experience and thousands of sessions have shaped an intuitive, evidence-informed approach that honors each client's unique anatomy and goals.

Myokinetic Touch was founded on a simple belief: the body remembers everything. Through skilled, intentional touch, we create the conditions for lasting relief, restoration, and resilience — not just temporary comfort.

Whether you're recovering from injury, managing chronic tension, preparing for an event, or simply investing in your wellbeing, sessions at Myokinetic Touch are calibrated to exactly where you are today.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

What modalities do you offer? +
We offer Swedish, deep tissue, sports massage, prenatal, myofascial release, and lymphatic drainage. Most sessions blend multiple techniques based on your needs that day.
How do I pay for my session? +
We accept Zelle and Venmo for easy, contactless payment. Payment is collected at the time of service.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend 48–72 hours in advance for best availability. Same-week appointments are sometimes available — check the online scheduler for current openings.
Is prenatal massage safe? +
Yes. After the first trimester, prenatal massage is safe and highly beneficial when performed by a trained therapist. We use specialized positioning and gentle, pregnancy-appropriate techniques.
What should I do before my first appointment? +
Arrive well hydrated, avoid heavy meals for about an hour beforehand, and be ready to share your health history and goals. A brief intake conversation starts every first session.
How often should I get a massage? +
For general wellness, monthly sessions are a solid baseline. For chronic pain or athletic recovery, bi-weekly or even weekly sessions often produce more lasting results. We'll advise based on your goals.
