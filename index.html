About Me

Precision, presence, and purpose

My journey into massage therapy began in May 2009, growing into a full-time practice in 2010. Over 16 years of experience and thousands of sessions have shaped an intuitive, evidence-informed approach that honors each client's unique anatomy and goals.

Myokinetic Touch was founded on a simple belief: the body remembers everything. Through skilled, intentional touch, we create the conditions for lasting relief, restoration, and resilience — not just temporary comfort.

Whether you're recovering from injury, managing chronic tension, preparing for an event, or simply investing in your wellbeing, sessions at Myokinetic Touch are calibrated to exactly where you are today.

What I Offer

Modalities tailored to your body

No single technique fits every body. Every session begins with a conversation about your goals, history, and current state — then draws on the modalities best suited to your needs that day.

Relaxation

Swedish Massage

Classic long strokes, kneading, and rhythmic tapotement to promote full-body relaxation, improve circulation, and ease generalized tension.

Therapeutic

Deep Tissue

Slow, deliberate pressure targeting deeper muscle layers and fascia to address chronic tension patterns, adhesions, and postural imbalances.

Performance

Sports Massage

Pre- and post-event techniques to prepare muscles for performance, accelerate recovery, and reduce injury risk for athletes of all levels.

Specialized

Prenatal Massage

Gentle, safe techniques designed specifically for expecting mothers to relieve back pain, reduce swelling, and support overall wellbeing during pregnancy.

Structural

Myofascial Release

Sustained pressure and gentle stretching to release fascial restrictions, restore range of motion, and reduce pain throughout the myofascial network.

Restorative

Lymphatic Drainage

Very light, rhythmic strokes following lymphatic pathways to reduce inflammation, support immune function, and accelerate post-surgical recovery.
